20th Anniversary

Event Date: December 2, 2023

The Computer Science Department's 20th anniversary is an important milestone that merits consideration and celebration. The department has been at the vanguard of influencing technology and education during the past 20 years, generating highly qualified individuals and advancing the subject. This memorial service provides a chance to honor the department's development from its founding to its current status as a center of academic excellence, creativity, and knowledge. The department's 20-year anniversary represents not just the passage of time but also its expansion, accomplishments, and influence on teachers, students, and the community at large.On top of that, the 20th anniversary offers a forum for expressing appreciation. A sense of community and pride is fostered by acknowledging the contributions made by the faculty, staff, students, and alumni who have been crucial to the department's success. It's an opportunity to share success stories, draw attention to noteworthy accomplishments, and express appreciation for the cooperative spirit that has shaped the culture of the department.

Awards Night

Event Date: December 10, 2023

The awards night for the computer science department is a much-anticipated occasion that honors academic success, inventiveness, and the efforts of staff, teachers, and students. This yearly event is a significant occasion where the department's commitment and hard work are acknowledged. Department heads usually provide a warm welcome to kick off awards night, laying the groundwork for an evening full of gratitude and recognition. It offers a venue for showcasing the extraordinary abilities and successes of people who have achieved success in a range of computer science-related fields.

Past Events

Global CyberTech Workshop

Event Date: August 26, 2023

The Computer Science Department's Global Cybertech Workshop is a ground-breaking program that brings together professionals, academics, and industry experts to examine and discuss the constantly changing opportunities and challenges in the field of technology and cybersecurity. Participants from various backgrounds can collaborate and share knowledge by using this workshop as a forum. A range of interesting events are usually included, such as panel discussions, interactive technology demonstrations, hands-on technical workshops, and keynote speeches by well-known cybersecurity experts. Improving participants' knowledge of current cybersecurity trends, dangers, and innovations is one of the workshop's main goals. This is accomplished through comprehensive technical seminars that address a variety of subjects, such as penetration testing and ethical hacking to secure coding practices and threat intelligence.
