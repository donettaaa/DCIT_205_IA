Admissions Information

The demand for qualified experts in the field of computer science is reflected in the extremely tough admissions process for this department. Because computer science is an analytical and problem-solving field, prospective students usually need to show that they have a solid academic background in science and mathematics.Embark on a journey of knowledge and innovation. Join our elite Computer Science Department.

Admission Requirements

  • Strong academic background in mathematics and science
  • Completion of relevant coursework
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Compelling personal statement
  • All applicants will be considered on the basis of result equivalents as provided by the Ghana Tertiary Education Commission(GTEC), as per schedule below:

    WASSCE Grade & Interpretation

  • A1 - Excellent
  • B2 - Very Good
  • B3 - Good
  • C3 - Good
  • C4 - Credit
  • C4 - Credit
  • C5 - Credit
  • C6 - Credit
  • D7 - Pass
  • E8 - Pass
  • F9 - Fail
